Our Mission

Transforming medical education through innovative technology

Who We Are

PharmEd was founded in 2015 by a team of medical educators and technologists who saw a need for better learning tools in healthcare education.

Our platform combines evidence-based medical content with cutting-edge learning technology to create an engaging, effective educational experience for students and professionals alike.

Today, we serve over 50,000 healthcare learners worldwide, from medical students to practicing physicians and pharmacists.
