Metformin

Antidiabetic
Indications & Dosage

Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus: 500 mg once or twice daily, max 2000 mg/day.

PCOS: Off-label use for insulin resistance.

Adverse Effects
  • GI upset (diarrhea, nausea)
  • Vitamin B12 deficiency (long-term)
  • Lactic acidosis (rare)
Drug Interactions
  • Contrast dye: Risk of lactic acidosis. Hold 48h before/after.
  • Alcohol: ↑ Risk of hypoglycemia.

Ibuprofen

NSAID
Key Facts

Pain/Fever: 200–400 mg every 4–6 hours (max 1200 mg/day).

⚠️ Warning: Avoid in peptic ulcer disease, CKD, or with anticoagulants.
